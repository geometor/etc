Notes

In the plane of a triangle ABC, let Ia = line through X(7) parallel to BC, and define Ib and Ic cyclically. Ac = Ia&cap;AB, and define Ba and Cb cyclically. Ab = Ia&cap;AC, and define Bc and Ca cyclically. Oa = circumcircle of A, Bc, Cb, and define Ob and Oc cyclically. Then

X(77) = radical center of Oa, Ob, Oc. See also X(77). (Ivan Pavlov, April 1, 2022)
