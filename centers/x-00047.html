Notes

Let A’B’C’ be the Kosnita triangle. Let A&Prime; be the trilinear product B’*C’, and define B&Prime;, C&Prime; cyclically. The lines AA&Prime;, BB&Prime;, CC&Prime; concur in X(47). (Randy Hutson, March 21, 2019)

Let A’B’C’ and A&Prime;B&Prime;C&Prime; be the Lucas and Lucas(-1) tangents triangles. Let A* be the trilinear product A’A&Prime;, and define B, C* cyclically. The lines AA*, BB*, CC* concur in X(47). (Randy Hutson, March 21, 2019)
