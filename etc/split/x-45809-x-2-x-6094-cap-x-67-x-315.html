 Tangents from A1, B1, C1 to circles (A1B2C2), (B1C2A2), (C1A2B2) bound a triangle T'=A'B'C'.
 Tangents from A2, B2, C2 to circles (A2B1C1), (B2C1A1), (C2A1B1) bound a triangle T"=A"B"C".
