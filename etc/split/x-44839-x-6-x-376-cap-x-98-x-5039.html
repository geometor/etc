Perspectors involving 8th and 9th mixtilinear triangles: X(44840)-X(44859)

This preamble and centers X(44840)-X(44859) were contributed by César Eliud Lozada, September 18, 2021.

Dan Reznik defines 8th and 9th mixtilinear triangles as follows:

  • Let ω'a, ω'b and ω'c be the A-, B- and C- mixtilinear incircles of ABC, and Ωi the inner Apollonius circle of them. Denote as A', B', C' the touchpoints of Ωi and ω'a, ω'b and ω'c, respectively. The triangle A'B'C' is the 8th-mixtilinear triangle of ABC.
  • Let ω"a, ω"b and ω"c be the A-, B- and C- mixtilinear excircles of ABC, and Ωo the outer Apollonius circle of them. Denote as A", B", C" the touchpoints of Ωo and ω"a, ω"b and ω"c, respectively. The triangle A"B"C" is the 9th-mixtilinear triangle of ABC.

Barycentric coordinates of the A-vertices of these triangles are:

  • 8th mixtilinear triangle: A' = a*(a^2 + 2*(b + c)*a - 3*(b - c)^2)/2 : b^2*(a - b + c) : c^2*(a + b - c)
  • 9th mixtilinear triangle: A" = a*(a^2 - 2*(b + c)*a - 3*(b - c)^2)/2 : b^2*(a + b - c) : c^2*(a - b + c)
